About the Author

BirkleyGrunewald

Writer  ·  Researcher  ·  Correspondent

These papers represent an ongoing attempt to think clearly about politics, economy, and the structures that shape how people live. Written across years of study and travel, they range from formal academic arguments to shorter reflections on questions that resist easy answers.

The work sits alongside photography, travel writing, and short fiction — each form asking a different kind of attention from the same underlying preoccupations.
